Got Questions?

We've got answers.

With regular VR you're fully isolated in a digital world. With Mixed Reality (using the Meta Quest 3), you still see the real world around you — people, the room, everything — but digital objects and characters appear layered on top of it in real time. You stay aware of your surroundings, which makes it safer, more social, and far easier for anyone to pick up and play.

As little as 3m × 3m. That's enough for our Compact Zone setup — suitable for classrooms, corridors, and mall booths. For a fuller event experience with a backdrop, leaderboard screen, and crowd-viewing area, 5m × 5m is ideal. We also offer an inflatable tent option for outdoor activations.
